- About -

- Get to know me -

My name is Natalia Cegielska and I am a polish artist.

I was born in Wroclaw in Lower Silesia region and have been living here almost my entire life.

I studied in Wroclaw's Academy of Fine Art where I received my BA in 2011. I was drawing and painting since I was a child, but really started painting full-time in 2013.

In order to escape the fast-paced environment of the big city, I have moved to the north-east part of the country, where I live close to nature. I enjoy the silence and peace, as it helps me to think clear and to create new art.

Whenever I don't paint, I travel the world, always looking for inspirations. I love sailing and wall climbing, as it gives me just the right amount of adrenaline I need to stay focused.

My work is displayed in galleries and public space across the country.

- Inspiration -

Abstract expressionism is the trend in art that best describes what I do.

All of my ideas are coming from the surroundings, from my travels and - most of all - the people I meet along the way.

I am trying to capture the movement and emotions through my work and each of my pieces displays different emotions I had at the time, hence you will never find two pieces the same

I always look at things from different perspectives and with a great dose of empathy.

I see the light in people and the darkness in places.

This balanced world is full of joy, happiness and love, but at the same time we are all fighting our demons. This state of things always casues mixed emotions.

That - in turn - gives me fresh ideas and never ending stories that people and places can tell through my work.

My technique and style has been changing slightly over the years, but all my work is acrylic painting on canvas, with a use of the brush & spatula technique.

I encourage you to have look at my work.
